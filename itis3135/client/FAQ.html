Is there a Price?

There is no price associated with this service. Everything in this site is for free.

What is the Test Like?

The test will consist of 10 questions. Each question will be worth 1 point and they can range from simply declaring and initializing a variable to creating an entire function.

When should I receive my Score?

You will receive your score as soon as you complete the test. If you do not like your score, you can take the test again.

Can I go at my own Pace?

Yes, you can. We recommend for you to take your time when trying to learn Python. This is not a race so take all the time you need to learn.
